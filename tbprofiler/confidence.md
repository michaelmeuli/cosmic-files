## WHO mutation catalogue
In 2021 the WHO published a list of mutations associated with drug resistance in TB. 
This was followed up in 2023 with a more comprehensive list and can be found [here](https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240082410).

- Group 1: Assoc with R
- Group 2: Assoc with R–interim
- Group 3: Uncertain significance
- Group 4: Not assoc with R–interim
- Group 5: Not assoc with R
